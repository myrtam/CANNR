Vaccination Example

This Web app illustrates the use of the CANNRTM Analytics Container Building Tool. This example involves a hypothetical scenario in which a vaccine is being offered to the public to control a viral pandemic.

The app schedules people for the vaccine by collecting their information and then calling a REST service exposed by CannR to decide whether someone can be scheduled to receive the vaccine immediately or should be placed on a waiting list. The REST service exposes models of mortality and life expectence provided as simple Python functions.

The models in this example are strictly hypothetical.
